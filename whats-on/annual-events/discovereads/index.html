I suggest that reading fiction, that reading for pleasure, is one of the most important things one can do. — Neil Gaiman

Discovereads is a new reading promotion which aims to get people reading something a bit different. The six Discovereads books are: - I Am, I Am, I Am, by Maggie O’Farrell - The Graveyard Book, by Neil Gaiman & Chris Riddell - The Great Level, by Stella Tillyard - Persepolis, by Marjane Satrapi - The Shepherd’s Hut, by Tim Winton - The Sisters Brothers, by Patrick DeWitt
